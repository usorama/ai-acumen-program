OpenAI: The Reasoning Engine

🧠 Core Philosophy: Advanced Reasoning

OpenAI focuses on building AI that can think through complex problems step-by-step, similar to human reasoning processes.

Key Strengths (2025):

  • o-series Models: Advanced reasoning and planning capabilities
  • Creative Generation: Writing, art, code with high quality
  • Agentic Capabilities: AI that can take actions and make decisions
  • Research Integration: Deep Research agent for complex analysis

💡 Best Use Cases

Complex problem-solving, strategic research, creative content generation, code development, M&A analysis

Google: The Integrated Workflow

⚙️ Core Philosophy: Seamless Integration

Google's AI is designed to work within existing business workflows, especially Google Workspace ecosystem.

Key Strengths (2025):

  • Workspace Integration: Native AI in Gmail, Docs, Sheets
  • Multimodal Capabilities: Text, images, video in one model
  • Enterprise Agents: Business process automation
  • Real-time Collaboration: AI-enhanced team productivity

💡 Best Use Cases

Business process automation, collaborative content creation, data analysis in spreadsheets

Anthropic: The Trusted Partner

🛡️ Core Philosophy: Safety & Reliability

Anthropic prioritizes constitutional AI with built-in safety measures and transparent reasoning processes for enterprise trust.

Key Strengths (2025):

  • Large Context Window: 200K+ tokens for comprehensive document analysis
  • Low Hallucination Rate: Most accurate responses for factual queries
  • Constitutional AI: Built-in ethical guidelines and safety measures
  • Transparent Reasoning: Shows work and explains decision-making process

💡 Best Use Cases

Legal document analysis, financial compliance, regulatory research, sensitive data processing

Exercise: Platform-Problem Fit

🎯 Interactive Challenge (5 minutes)

Instructions: Match each business scenario to the optimal AI platform. Consider the unique strengths we just discussed.

Scenario 1: M&A Due Diligence

Your team needs to analyze 500+ legal documents, contracts, and financial reports for a potential acquisition. Accuracy is critical.

Best Platform: Anthropic Claude - Large context window, low hallucination rate

Scenario 2: Creative Marketing Campaign

Marketing needs to generate 50 unique slogans and ad copy variations for a new product launch with innovative messaging.

Best Platform: OpenAI GPT - Advanced creative generation and reasoning

Scenario 3: Automated Reporting

Operations needs AI to automatically generate weekly reports from Google Sheets data and share via Gmail.

Best Platform: Google Gemini - Native Workspace integration

Decision Framework

Step 1: Define the Problem

  • What type of task? (Analysis, Creation, Automation)
  • What's the required accuracy level?
  • How complex is the reasoning needed?
  • What systems need integration?

Step 2: Match Platform Strengths

  • Complex Analysis: Anthropic
  • Creative Work: OpenAI
  • Workflow Integration: Google
  • Hybrid Needs: Multi-platform approach

💭 Reflection Exercise

Think of one specific AI use case in your department. Which platform would you choose and why? Write down your reasoning using the decision framework above.

Key Takeaways

🎯 Strategic Insights from Session 1

Core Learning: AI platform selection is a strategic decision that should align with specific business needs and organizational context.

1. Platform Awareness

Each AI ecosystem has distinct strengths: OpenAI for reasoning, Google for integration, Anthropic for reliability.

2. Strategic Matching

Success comes from matching platform capabilities to specific business problems, not choosing based on hype.

3. Decision Framework

Use systematic evaluation: problem type, accuracy needs, integration requirements, and organizational fit.
