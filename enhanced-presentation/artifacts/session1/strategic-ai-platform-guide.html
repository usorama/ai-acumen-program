üéÆ Practice Scenarios

Scenario 1: M&A Due Diligence

Your team needs to analyze 500+ legal documents, financial statements, and contracts for a potential acquisition. Time-sensitive with high accuracy requirements.

Recommended Solution: Anthropic Claude
Reasoning: Large context window handles multiple documents simultaneously, low hallucination rate critical for financial accuracy, constitutional AI ensures consistent analysis approach.
Scenario 2: Creative Marketing Campaign

Launch campaign for new product targeting Gen Z consumers. Need innovative concepts, social media content, and video scripts across multiple platforms.

Recommended Solution: OpenAI GPT-4
Reasoning: Superior creative capabilities, understanding of cultural trends, ability to generate diverse content formats, strong brand voice consistency.
Scenario 3: Automated Reporting

Monthly sales reports combining data from Google Sheets, requiring charts, analysis, and distribution to 50+ stakeholders via email.

Recommended Solution: Google Gemini
Reasoning: Native Sheets integration, automated email distribution through Gmail, real-time data processing, enterprise security compliance.
