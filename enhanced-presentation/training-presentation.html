The Three AI Ecosystems

Each major AI platform has a distinct philosophy that determines their strengths and ideal use cases.

OpenAI: "The Reasoning Engine"

Philosophy: Push the frontier of raw intelligence and reasoning capabilities

2025 Focus: o-series models with advanced reasoning, creative generation, agentic workflows

Best For: Complex problem-solving, research, strategic analysis

Google: "The Integrated Workflow"

Philosophy: Seamless integration with existing productivity tools

2025 Focus: Workspace integration, enterprise agents, multimodal capabilities

Best For: Business process automation, collaborative content creation

Anthropic: "The Trusted Partner"

Philosophy: Safety, reliability, and constitutional AI principles

2025 Focus: Large context windows, low hallucination rates, enterprise safety

Best For: Legal/financial analysis, compliance monitoring, sensitive data

The AI ROI Reality Check

Most organizations struggle to demonstrate tangible business value from AI investments.

The Challenge

  • Only 17% of organizations see tangible EBIT impact from AI
  • 80% struggle to demonstrate business value from GenAI
  • Success requires strategic approach, not just technology deployment

The Solution

  • Companies with formal AI strategies see 80% success rates
  • Without strategy: only 37% success rate
  • Focus on transformation, not just efficiency

🎯 Speaker Notes:

Key Message: "AI ROI isn't just about efficiency - it's about transformation. We need to think beyond 'how much time did we save' to 'how much better are our decisions.'"
